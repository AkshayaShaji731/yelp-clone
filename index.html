Monty's Sunset - South Beach

When I lived in Miami many years ago, we were on a budget and loved going yo happy hour. I remember ordering from raw bar and that their nachos were huge and filling. The sunset views on the marina were to die for! On a recent trip, I couldn't figure out where to go that wasn't too fancy but would have good food and decided to try Monty's. The boat show was in town, and that was stunning to see. I was being very indecisive about what to eat, so I went with the fisherman's sampler. It was HUGE - soooo much fried seafood! I originally want the calamari, conch fritters, and coconut shrimp, but the standout was actually the cod. I would say this could feed at least six people with a salad too. My server disappeared for about half an hour after I was done, so I would say believe the reviews about the service. I'm sure there are better places than Monty's, but the view of marina can't be beat.
